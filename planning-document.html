Planning Document

Site Purpose

The purpose of my site is to provide women with fashion ideas. It will include photos of outfits and will provide links when you click on the image that will direct you to the site to which you can buy it. It will be much like pintrest or a fashion blog.

Target Audience

The audience is women who are looking for inspiration to create the best outfit possible. It will probably be younger women who are teenagers and young adults, who are more in tune with the current trends and are looking at where to buy trendy items.

Persona

Abish is a 21 year old female from San Jose, California. She is a student at Stanford University studying Web Design and is in tune with the current trends. Abish is looking for websites to where she can buy these trendy looks for the upcoming semester.
