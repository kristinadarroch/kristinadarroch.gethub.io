My Mobile Browsing Experience

Visitor Analysis and Persona

amazon.com

URL: https://www.amazon.com

Visitor Analysis

The website Amazon (an electronic commerce company) does a great job in providing the customer with a great mobile experience. This is shown greatly in the design of the user experience. The website uses great contrast of color of keeping the same tones throughout the main webpage, staying to similar colors and tones to display menus and information to find various content on the web site while using brighter colors to display things that might interest the customer. Alignment is seen throughout the web page as you seamlessly scroll down the main page.

Persona

A persona developed for this website is a middle aged woman named Bertha, looking for a cardigan to help keep warm during the fall months. The major needs of this customer would be the quality of the product and some reviews to see how the cardigan held up in the weather and after constant use. The users expectations will be high considering the advertisement and popularity of this website. The user will be looking for an easy and fast shopping experience that yeilds a great deal compared to shopping in local stores.

Site Contrast

1. Did they or did they not know who their target audience was?

Yes. This website did a great job in displaying what the target audiece would be by displaying a good and clean tones that would appeal to any user, but most importantly, the user discribed above.

2. Did they or did they not meet the needs of their audience?

Yes. The website did meet the needs of the audience by having an easy experience in finding the item that was needed.

3. Did they or did they not organize content to facilitate it being found?

They organized the content very well by displaying the most popular items first and also provided many other options to help narrow down my search.

4. Did the site facilitate visitors in a mobile environment?

Yes. The site did a great job in providing a very organized and easy mobile experience for buying products.
